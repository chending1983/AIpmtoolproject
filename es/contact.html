Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

我们大多数产品的标准最小起订量是1吨。但是，我们可以接受样品或试批的小订单。请联系我们的销售团队了解具体要求。

Do you provide product samples?

Yes, we provide samples for evaluation purposes. Sample quantities typically range from 1-5 kg depending on the product. Please contact us to request samples.

What are your payment terms?

我们接受电汇（T/T）、信用证（L/C）和其他标准国际付款方式。具体条款可以根据订单量和客户关系进行讨论。

How long is your delivery time?

Standard delivery time is 2-4 weeks after order confirmation, depending on product availability and destination. Custom orders may require additional lead time.

Do you offer custom specifications?

Yes, we can customize our products to meet your specific requirements including particle size, surface area, pore volume, and other properties. Please contact our technical team to discuss your needs.
